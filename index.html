RABIES

PREVENTABLE YET

DEADLY

The deadly virus that destroys the nervous system.

About 59,000 people around the world die each year from rabies.

SYMPTOMS

While most people know the name of the disease, many are still unaware of its effects on the human body.

By the time the symptoms appear, the disease is already 100% fatal.

CAUSES

While rabies can be caused by several factors, it is mostly transmitted through contact with infected animals. The most common ones include:



• Being infected animal bites and scratches.

• Spread with saliva.

• By pets and farm animals: cows, dogs, cats, horses.

• By wild animals: bats, foxes, wolves, monkeys, and coyotes.

IN CASE OF

EXPOSURE

Do not panic! In cases where you have had contact with infected animals, follow the two steps below right away.

Wash the exposed surface for at least 15 minutes.

Drive to your local hospital for immediate vaccination.

PREVENT IT

The best thing to prevent such problem is to first eliminate any possibility of the disease infecting you. For this, several easy steps can be taken:



• Ensure that your pets are up-to-date with their rabies vaccinations.

• Do not approach or provoke wild and stray animals carelessly.

• Vaccinate yourself.

• Detect and alert new potential cases as early as possible.

• Do not let your pets roam without supervision outside.

Protect yourself, your health, and your life.

Before its too late.
